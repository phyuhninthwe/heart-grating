Feature

Heart Grating is high quality, low price and manufactured by state-of-the-art welding machine at Vietnam factory with JIS (Japan Industrial Standard) qualified raw material and under strict quality control.

We are manufacturers specializing in steel grating and our products ("Heart Grating") are consumed in Japan, Canada, USA, Australia and Vietnam. Heart Grating is highly used in infrastructure works such as road, high way, parking space, condominium, factory, power plant and incinerators.

Steel Bar Grating is extremely strong and durable for all load bearing applications and is used for pedestrian, light vehicle traffic and industrial flooring. Steel Bar Grating can be easily fabricated to nearly any configuration. The high percentage of open area makes bar grating practically maintenance free and all products are fully recyclable.

Steel bar grating is available in a variety of bearing bar spacing and thickness depending on applications and load requirements. Available in either ① non-serrated surface or ② serrated for slip resistance. Finish options include (i) bare steel, (ii) painted red, (iii) coated black, or (iv) hot dip galvanized. The height of bearing bar: 19mm(3/4”), 25mm(1”), 32mm(1-1/4”), 38mm(1-1/2”), 44mm(1-3/4”), 50mm(2”), 55mm(2-3/16”), 60mm(2-3/8”), 65mm(2-5/8”), 75mm(3”), 90mm(3-1/2”). The thickness of bearing bar: 3mm(1/8”), 4.5mm(3/16”), 6mm(1/4”), 9mm(3/8”).
